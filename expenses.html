ARI Reimbursement Policy
⚠ Receipt Requirement (IRS Mandatory)
All receipts must show: date of service, provider name, service performed, and amount. Payment confirmation receipts alone are not accepted.
🏥 HRA Pool — Dental, Vision & HSA

ARI funds your HSA account 4× per year via your HRA allowance. Total HRA allowance is $20,000/year. After the HSA contribution is made, the remainder covers dental and vision.

HSA max — Single plan (2026)$4,400
HSA max — Family plan (2026)$8,750
Remaining for dental & vision (family example)~$11,250

HSA contribution schedule: 2nd payroll in February · Q1 bonus · Q2 bonus · Q3 bonus

Dental
  • Exams & x-rays
  • Crowns & fillings
  • Braces (ortho)
  • Mouth guards
  • Root canals
Vision
  • Exams & testing
  • Glasses
  • Contact lenses & solution
  • Specialty services (confirm with Melissa)
📚 CME Pool — Business & Education Allowance
Flat-Rate Reimbursements
  • Cell phone: $90/mo ($270/qtr) — submit 1 invoice/yr
  • Home internet: $100/mo ($300/qtr) — submit 1 invoice/yr
  • Cell/tablet equipment: 1 device per 2 years
  • Computer/laptop: up to $2,000, every 3 years
Other Eligible Expenses
  • NORS, OSMA, RSNA, ARRS, Summit County Medical Society dues
  • Home office equipment (desk, chair, lighting, keyboard, headphones, etc.)
  • Generator or UPS battery backup
  • Campus parking (paid lots)
  • Disability insurance premiums
🏛 Corporate Expenses — Billed directly to ARI

These required expenses are covered by ARI and do not come out of your HRA or CME pools.

  • DEA License renewal
  • Hospital Staff Privileges
  • Ohio State Medical License
  • Identity Theft Protection (up to $250/yr)
Policy approved Nov 17, 2016 · updated June 2026. Questions? Contact Melissa.
